How to Resize Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Set your desired dimensions (width and height in pixels)
  3. Choose output format and quality settings
  4. Click "Resize Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your resized image

Why Resize Images?

  • Prepare photos for social media platforms
  • Reduce file size for email attachments
  • Create thumbnails or preview images
  • Standardize image sizes for consistency
